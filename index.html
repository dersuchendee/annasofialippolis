Work

I'm currently working as a project assistant at Vulci nel Mondo, but here are some of the projects I've been working on in the last year.

Variants Mining

A quantitative analysis of authorial corrections. The case of Alessandro Manzoni's I promessi sposi.
About the project

Digital WHOmanities

A cycle of seminars and workshops on the present and the future of the Digital Humanities organized by a group of DH students (FICLIT project of excellence 2020).
About the project

OpenSisma

An Open Data research project about the effects of the 2012 Earthquakes in Emilia-Romagna. The websites displays and maps relevant information on the buildings affected by the Earthquakes and investigates the correlation between damage index and school scores.
